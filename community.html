A living database requires a living community. This is where you come in.

Our Vision: A Collaborative Legal Ecosystem

Legislation is not static. It changes weekly through amendments, regulations, and court judgments. Keeping an open-access database current is a monumental task for one team, but achievable for a dedicated community. We invite legal professionals, students, activists, and engaged citizens to become part of the 'Community Watch'. Your collective knowledge is our greatest asset.

Lawyers & Advocates

Help interpret new judgments. Explain the practical impact of new amendments. Share your insights on our forums.

Law Students

Help track changes in the Government Gazette. Summarize new bills. Earn credit and build your public profile.

Engaged Citizens

Spot outdated information. Ask questions that highlight gaps in public knowledge. Help translate legal jargon into plain language.

Project Managers

Help track the progress of new bills through parliament and give feedback to the community.

Risk Managers

Anticipate the real-world impact of upcoming legal changes on businesses and individuals.

Developers

Contribute to the open-source codebase on GitHub. Help build new tools and improve functionality.
